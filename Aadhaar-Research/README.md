# 🆔 Aadhaar Update Coverage & Biometric Compliance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive state-wise analytical study of Aadhaar enrolment, demographic updates, and mandatory biometric compliance across India. Using **UIDAI Open Data**, the analysis identifies regional disparities and behavioral patterns in update compliance, providing a data-driven perspective on India's digital identity infrastructure.

---

## 🎯 Analytical Focus Areas
*   **Total Aadhaar Enrolment**: Mapping the penetration of digital identity across diverse Indian states.
*   **Mandatory Biometric Updates (MBU)**: Tracking compliance for the critical age group of 5–17 years.
*   **Update Intensity Metrics**: Calculating the ratio of updates per enrolled resident to identify administrative efficiency.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation (BI Excellence)
As a **Microsoft Certified: PL-300** analyst, I applied advanced BI techniques to this public dataset:
*   **Data Orchestration**: Cleaned and structured **UIDAI Open Government Data** using Power Query.
*   **DAX Modeling**: Engineered measures to calculate 'Update Velocity' and 'Compliance Percentages' across states.
*   **Geospatial Visualization**: Utilized filled maps and shape maps to highlight regional performance clusters.

---

## 🧠 Strategic Research Insights
*   **Volume vs. Intensity**: While populous states like **Uttar Pradesh** and **Bihar** lead in total volume, certain smaller states exhibit higher **Update Intensity**, indicating superior administrative outreach.
*   **Demographic Mobility**: Analysis suggests that update behavior is significantly influenced by population mobility and local governance efficiency.
*   **MBU Compliance**: Identified specific regional gaps in mandatory biometric updates for minors, providing actionable insights for targeted UIDAI awareness campaigns.
